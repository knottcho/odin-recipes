How to Make Pasta

What is Pasta?

Similar to pizza in many ways. Tasty carbs, melty cheese, tomato sauce. Also a real winner for humanity.

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Basil Leaves
  • Spices: Salt, Pepper, Garlic, Red Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta for 10-12 minutes.
  2. Drain the pasta and add tomato sauce.
  3. Sprinkle with cheese.
  4. Season with basil leaves and spices.
  5. Serve with extra parmesan and enjoy!
