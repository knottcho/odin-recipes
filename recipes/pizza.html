How to Make Pizza

What is Pizza?

Pizza is one of humanity's greatest accomplishments. Delicious, cheesy, truly the perfect food.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Basil Leaves
  • Spices: Salt, Pepper, Red Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Form the dough into a circle.
  2. Add tomato sauce.
  3. Sprinkle with cheese.
  4. Season with basil leaves and spices.
  5. Cook on a pizza stone in a hot oven for 20 minutes.
  6. Slice and enjoy!
