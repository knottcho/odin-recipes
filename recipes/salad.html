How to Make Salad

What is Salad?

Similar to pizza in almost zero ways. I mean, there's a couple tomatoes here and cheese if you're lucky, but that's where the similarities end.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Dressing
  • Veggies of Your Choice

Steps

  1. Chop lettuce and vegetables into bite sized pieces.
  2. Toss lettuce and vegetables in a bowl with dressing.
  3. Sprinkle with cheese.
  4. Serve with extra parmesan and enjoy!
